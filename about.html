Bank of Thailand

Data Analyst Intern

While I was working there, I participated in the Telecommunication export value forecasting project. Telecommunication products play a huge role in stabilizing Thailand's economy, with it's value ranked between the top 10 sectors with highest export in the country. My task was to predict the export value of this sector using two forecasting methods, Time-Series forecasting and Machine Learning algorithms using the import value of relevant parts used to produce the items in the sector. Then all results were compared.
